## Usage of Demo

### Generate Sudoku

Generate `N` x `N` randomly ordered Sudoku.

```shell
$ ./demo 4

 2 4 | 1 3
 1 3 | 2 4
-----+-----
 4 2 | 3 1
 3 1 | 4 2
```

```shell
$ ./demo 9

-------+-------+-------

 3 1 4 | 6 9 5 | 2 7 8
 2 7 8 | 3 1 4 | 6 9 5
```
